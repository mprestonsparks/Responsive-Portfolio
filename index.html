About Me

I'm a 29-year old accountant from Nashville, TN transitioning into software development. I've worked across a wide range of industries, developing, implementing, and operating accounting systems for small to mid-sized companies. Unlike most developers, I'm familiar with the requirements of starting and running a business- dealing with customers, suppliers, government agencies and (occasionally conflicting) departmental needs.  

This experience allows me to work directly with business owners, employees and other stakeholders to build solutions which are designed to meet their individual needs.  

I'm a firm believer in being a life-long student and love to learn. Outside of software development and accounting, I enjoy studying a variety of topics, from psychology and philosophy to economics and religion.  

I love to travel when the opportunity arises, enjoy anything outdoors, and would like to one day become a writer.
